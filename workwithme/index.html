About Me

Work with me!

I've been freelancing full-time for just about 3 years now, and I've been lucky enough to be successful with it. My client base has grown large enough that I'm now unable to keep them all happy and still have a sustainable work-life balance. So, I'm looking to recruit fellow freelancers with space in their schedules, or day-jobbers looking for a side gig, to work with me and my clients on an as-needed basis. I'm targeting 300-500 hours of outsourced work in 2017, but the actual amount could vary widely based on the demands of my clients.

  • The majority of my work is in PHP (Zend Framework 2/3, Symfony 2) with a sprinkling of frontend stuff (AngularJS, mainly).
  • I prefer extending OSS/COTS projects rather than rolling my own from scratch (recent: OroPlatform, WHMCS, ReportServer, MODX, PimCore)
  • Flexible schedule; your own choice of platforms/languages; as long as the client is happy, I'm happy!
